# President

## Goal
Get rid of all your cards as fast as possible.


## Setup
- Deal all cards evenly.
- The player with the lowest card leads.

## Play
- Players must play the same number of cards with a higher rank.
- You may pass; passing skips you for the rest of the round.
- A round ends when all but one player pass.

## End
- First player out is President; last is Scum.
- Roles affect the next deal (optional).
