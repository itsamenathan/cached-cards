# Slapjack

## Goal
Win all the cards by slapping jacks.


## Setup
- Deal all cards evenly.
- Players keep their piles face down.

## Turn Order
- Players take turns flipping the top card to the center.
- When a jack appears, the first player to slap wins the pile.

## End
- First player to collect all cards wins.
