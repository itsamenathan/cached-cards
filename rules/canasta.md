# Canasta

## Goal
Score by forming melds and canastas (7-card melds).


## Setup
- Use 2 decks with jokers.
- Deal 11 cards each (more for 2 players); create stock and discard pile.

## Turn Order
1. Draw two cards from the stock or take the discard pile.
2. Meld sets of same rank.
3. Discard one card.

## Special Rules
- Jokers and 2s are wild.
- Each side must meet a minimum meld value to go down.

## End
- A team ends the hand by going out with a canasta.
