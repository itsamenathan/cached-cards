# Texas Hold'em

## Goal
Win chips by making the best 5-card hand or getting others to fold.


## Setup
- Each player gets 2 private cards.
- Five community cards are dealt in stages.

## Betting Rounds
1. **Pre-flop** (after hole cards)
2. **Flop** (3 community cards)
3. **Turn** (4th community card)
4. **River** (5th community card)

## Turn Actions
- **Check**, **Bet**, **Call**, **Raise**, or **Fold**.

## Showdown
- If more than one player remains, best 5-card hand wins.
