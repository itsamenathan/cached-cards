# Bridge

## Goal
Score points by fulfilling a bid (contract) in partnership.


## Setup
- Deal 13 cards to each player.

## Bidding
- Partners bid to set the contract: number of tricks and trump (or no trump).

## Play
- The player left of the declarer leads.
- Dummy hand is laid face up after the opening lead.
- Highest card of led suit wins unless trump is played.

## End
- Score based on whether the contract is made.
