# 500

## Goal
Reach 500 points by winning tricks based on your bid.


## Setup
- Deal 10 cards each for 4 players.
- A kitty of 3 cards is set aside.

## Bidding
- Players bid the number of tricks and trump suit.
- Highest bid becomes declarer and takes the kitty.

## Play
- Follow suit if possible.
- Highest card in the led suit wins unless trump is played.

## Scoring
- Make your bid to score; fail and you lose points.
