# Cribbage

## Goal
Reach 121 points by scoring during play and in hands.


## Setup
- Each player gets 6 cards.
- Discard 2 cards each to the crib.
- Turn a starter card face up.

## Play (Pegging)
- Players alternate laying cards, keeping a running total up to 31.
- Score points for 15s, pairs, and runs.

## Show
- Score your hand plus the starter card.
- Dealer also scores the crib.

## End
- First player to 121 wins.
