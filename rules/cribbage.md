# Cribbage

## Goal
Score points to reach 121.

## Key Terms
- **Crib:** Extra hand scored by the dealer.
- **Pegging:** Scoring while counting up to 31.
- **Starter:** The cut card added to all hands.


## Setup
- Deal 6 cards each.
- Each player discards 2 to the crib (dealerâ€™s bonus hand).
- Cut the starter card; dealer scores 2 for a jack (his heels).

## Pegging
- Players alternate laying cards without exceeding 31.
- Score for 15s, pairs, runs, and 31.

## Show
- Score each hand plus the starter card.
- Dealer scores the crib last.

## Example Pegging
You play a 7 (total 7). Opponent plays an 8 (total 15) and scores 2 points for 15.

## End
- First to 121 wins.
