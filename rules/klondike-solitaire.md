# Klondike Solitaire

## Goal
Move all cards to the four foundation piles.


## Setup
- Deal 7 tableau piles with 1â€“7 cards, only the top card face up.
- The rest form the stock.

## Play
- Build tableau piles in descending order, alternating colors.
- Build foundations from Ace to King by suit.
- Draw from the stock when no moves are available.

## End
- Win when all cards are on the foundations.
